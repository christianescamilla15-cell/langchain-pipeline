QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT - Q4 2024
Nextera Digital Holdings Inc.
Period: October 1 - December 31, 2024

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Nextera Digital Holdings delivered strong financial performance in Q4 2024, driven by
robust growth in our cloud services division and successful expansion into the APAC market.
Total revenue reached $128.5 million, representing a 23% year-over-year increase.

REVENUE BREAKDOWN
- Cloud Services: $72.3M (up 31% YoY) - Our flagship segment continues to benefit from
  enterprise migration trends and increased adoption of AI-powered solutions.
- Consulting: $34.2M (up 12% YoY) - Growth driven by demand for digital transformation
  advisory services.
- Licensing: $22.0M (up 8% YoY) - Steady performance with improved renewal rates at 94%.

PROFITABILITY
- Gross Profit: $89.9M (gross margin: 70.0%, up from 67.2%)
- Operating Income: $32.1M (operating margin: 25.0%)
- Net Income: $24.6M (net margin: 19.1%)
- EBITDA: $38.4M (EBITDA margin: 29.9%)

KEY METRICS
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR): $24.1M (up 28% YoY)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $3,200 (down 15% from Q3)
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): $48,500 (up 22% YoY)
- Net Revenue Retention: 118% (industry-leading)
- Employee Count: 1,247 (up from 1,089 in Q4 2023)

BALANCE SHEET HIGHLIGHTS
- Total Assets: $412M
- Cash & Equivalents: $89.2M
- Total Debt: $45.0M (debt-to-equity ratio: 0.18)
- Working Capital: $67.3M

OUTLOOK AND GUIDANCE
For FY 2025, we project revenue of $550-580M (28-35% growth), driven by continued cloud
adoption, the launch of our AI Analytics Platform in Q2, and expansion into European markets.
We expect operating margins to improve to 27-29% as we achieve greater economies of scale.

RISK FACTORS
- Macroeconomic uncertainty could impact enterprise IT spending
- Competitive pressure from hyperscaler cloud providers
- Talent acquisition challenges in key technical roles
- Regulatory changes in data privacy across operating jurisdictions

The Board has approved a share buyback program of up to $25M for FY 2025, reflecting
confidence in the company's growth trajectory and strong cash position.
