PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025,
by and between TechCorp Solutions Inc. ("Provider") and Global Enterprises LLC ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Provider agrees to deliver cloud infrastructure migration services, including assessment,
planning, execution, and post-migration support. The project timeline spans 6 months from
the effective date, with monthly milestone reviews.

2. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall pay Provider a total fee of $450,000, payable in monthly installments of $75,000.
Late payments shall incur a penalty of 1.5% per month. All invoices are due within 30 days
of receipt. Provider reserves the right to suspend services for payments overdue by 60 days.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information shared
during the engagement. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement for a period
of three (3) years. Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and
liability for damages.

4. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client.
Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Each party
agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other party against claims arising from its own
negligence or willful misconduct.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice. In the event of
material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately upon written notice.
Upon termination, Client shall pay for all services rendered through the termination date.

6. COMPLIANCE AND AUDIT
Provider shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards. Client
reserves the right to audit Provider's compliance with the terms of this Agreement upon
reasonable notice. Provider shall maintain accurate records for a period of five (5) years.

7. DEFAULT
In the event of default by either party, the non-defaulting party shall provide written notice
specifying the nature of the default. The defaulting party shall have 15 business days to
cure the default before any termination rights may be exercised.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to
conflicts of law principles.
